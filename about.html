Our Mission

Neon Circuits was born from a simple idea: technology should inspire wonder. In a world where tech blogs focus on specs and benchmarks, we explore the philosophical, artistic, and borderline surreal aspects of emerging technologies.

Founded in 2024 by a group of AI researchers and sci-fi enthusiasts, we bridge the gap between hardcore tech analysis and visionary futurism.
